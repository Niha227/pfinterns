Meal Prep Ideas for Busy Weekdays

Make your weekdays easier with these meal prep ideas that save time and keep you healthy. Meal prepping is a fantastic way to ensure you have nutritious meals ready to go, even on the busiest of days. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Quinoa
  • Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (salt, pepper, garlic powder)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. Season the chicken breasts with spices and a drizzle of olive oil. Place them on a baking sheet.

3. Cook the chicken in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until fully cooked.

4. While the chicken is baking, cook quinoa according to package instructions.

5. Steam or roast the mixed vegetables until tender.

6. Divide the chicken, quinoa, and vegetables into meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

7. Reheat and enjoy a quick and healthy meal throughout the week!
